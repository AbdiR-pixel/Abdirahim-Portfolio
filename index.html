FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions I receive as a freelance designer.

1. What services do you offer?
I offer UI/UX design, graphic design, web design, and branding services. This includes wireframing, prototyping, logo design, social media graphics, responsive website design, landing pages, Framer development, brand identity, and visual style guides.
2. How does the design process work?
My process has 5 phases: Research & Strategy → Concept & Ideation → Feedback & Refinement → Testing & Optimization → Launch & Delivery. I keep you involved throughout to ensure the final product matches your vision perfectly.
3. How long does a project usually take?
Timeline depends on scope. A logo design typically takes 3–5 days, a web design project 1–3 weeks, and a full branding project 2–4 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline estimate before we begin.
4. What do I need to provide before starting?
To get started, I'll need a project brief including your goals, target audience, preferred style references, and any existing brand assets. The more detail you share, the better I can tailor the design to your needs.
5. Do you offer revisions?
Yes! All packages include revision rounds. Starter includes 2 revisions, Pro includes 4, and Premium includes unlimited revisions until you're fully satisfied with the result.
6. How do I get started?
Simply fill out the contact form below or email me at youneedall705@gmail.com. I'll get back to you within 24 hours to discuss your project and how we can work together.
